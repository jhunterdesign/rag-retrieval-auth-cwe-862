Our Story & Mission

More than a smokehouse. A place built on family, flavor, and community.

Our Story

Big’s BBQ & Smokehouse started as a backyard smoke pit in Marietta, Georgia, where family and friends gathered every weekend around slow-smoked brisket, ribs, and laughter.

What began as a passion for bold southern flavors turned into a place where the community could come together over real food made with care.

What We Stand For

  • Family recipes passed down through generations
  • Slow-smoked meats prepared with patience and care
  • Hospitality that makes every guest feel at home
  • Quality ingredients and food safety standards
  • Serving and supporting our local community

Food Safety & Quality

We follow strict food handling and preparation standards to ensure every meal is served safely and at the highest quality.

Community Roots

Big’s BBQ proudly serves Atlanta, Decatur, and Marietta. Being part of the community is at the heart of what we do.
